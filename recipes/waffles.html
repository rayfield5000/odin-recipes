Waffles

Description

Recipe that uses whole wheat flour for a fluffy and delicious waffle. This can be enjoyed with maple and any toppings of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

    • Steps

    1. Preheat waffle iron. Beat eggs in large owl with hand beater until fluffy. eat in flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla, just until smooth.
    2. Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray. Pour mix onto hot waffle iron. Cook until golden brown. Serve hot.
